Operations Research

More than a course, an essential tool for every industrial engineer.

Operations Research is the use of mathematical models and algorithms in order to perform a process of decision making, allows the analysis of decision taking into account the scarcity of resources, to determine how you can optimize a defined objective as maximizing profits or minimizing costs. Operations Research is the application of scientific methodology through mathematical models to represent the problem first and then to solve them.
